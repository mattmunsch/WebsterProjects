About Adam Sandler

  • Born: September 8, 1966
  • Place of Birth: Brooklyn, New York
  • Education: New York University

Adam Sandler got his start playing "Smitty" on The Cosby Show. In 1990, Sandler hit it big when he was hired as writer on the hit sketch comedy show, Saturday Night Live. Since that time, he has acted in more than 70 films, wrote 24 films, and produced 53.
